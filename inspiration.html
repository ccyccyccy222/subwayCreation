灵感

您可以在这里发表您的灵感，体验其他用户真实的灵感

“Design is a life attitude of pursuing perfection, and design is a life concept of pursuing taste. Let the inspiration be released freely and create a space for spiritual desire ” — Ferdinand A. Porsche

“Creativity is just connecting things. When you ask creative people how they did something, they feel a little guilty because they didn’t really do it, they just saw something. It seemed obvious to them after a while.” — LeBron Jobs

“灵感来源自生活中的每一处细节，在这个浮躁的世界，我们需要静下心来，慢慢去体会它， 你会发现生活中细微的美好，这就是我设计的理念。”— 周淑芬

“正是因为经常保持好奇心，不断积累知识；而去探求新思路，去运用所得的知识；一旦产生小的灵感，相信它的价值，并锲而不舍地把它发展下去。” — 刘伟豪

“I think design would be better if designers were much more skeptical about its applications. If you believe in the potency of your craft, where you choose to dole it out is not something to take lightly.” — Frank Chimero

“Design must be functional and functionality must be translated into visual aesthetics, without any reliance on gimmicks that have to be explained.” — Ferdinand A. Porsche
